Our Jagatpura branch brings Fankaar's Kathak training to fast-growing southeast Jaipur. Evening batches run three days a week and are taught personally by founder Vartika Tiwari — the same guru-shishya attention, close to home for families in Jagatpura, Model Town and the university belt.

Beginners are welcome round the year; students progress through the Prarambhik → Praveshika → Visharad exam stages at their own pace.
