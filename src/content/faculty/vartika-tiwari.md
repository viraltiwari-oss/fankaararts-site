**Mrs. Vartika Tiwari** is an elite Kathak exponent and the visionary Founder of *Fankaar Institute of Performing Arts*. With over 20 years of dedicated training, her artistic foundation was molded under the legendary Pt. Girdhari Maharaj of the Jaipur Gharana and further honed by the eminent Guru Ishira Parikh. Under her leadership, Fankaar has grown exponentially from a humble cohort of four students into a premier multi-branch dance academy across Jaipur. A distinguished scholar and performer recognized by the Ministry of Culture, Vartika combines pristine classical lineage with a modern commitment to preserving and propagating Indian classical arts.
