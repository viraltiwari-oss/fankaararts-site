**Mr. Rahul Bhalia** is a premier vocal artist and classical fusion vocalist based in Jaipur. He initiated his musical training under the tutelage of Guruji Shree Balveer Singh Ji Shekhawat, undergoing a rigorous 10-year mentorship in classical music. He is the founder and frontman of *Neer*, an award-winning Indian classical fusion band established in 2016. Celebrated for his exceptional versatility across diverse musical genres, Rahul blends deeply rooted classical traditions with modern arrangements. He is a graded artist for All India Radio and frequently serves as a premier stage performer and competitive jury member across regional and international platforms.
